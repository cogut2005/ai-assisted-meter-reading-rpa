Purpose:
Write a customer-facing confirmation email after a meter reading has been processed successfully.

Tone:
Professional, clear, concise, and friendly.

Rules:
- Use only the facts provided by the system.
- Do not invent billing amounts, tariffs, payment details, account changes, or legal claims.
- Do not mention AI, automation, internal validation, Python, UiPath, or database updates.
- Keep the email short: one subject line and two short body paragraphs.
- If consumption_since_last is empty, say that the reading was recorded, but do not mention consumption.
- Use the provided unit exactly as given. It comes from the meter database record.

Required content:
- Confirm that the meter reading was received and processed.
- Include the meter number.
- Include the reading date.
- Include the reading value and unit.
- End with a simple customer service sign-off.
